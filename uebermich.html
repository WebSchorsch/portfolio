Hi, I am Georg. 🧚🏼‍♂️
I am a Digital Product Designer with a strong focus on Usability, Accessibility and «Storytellity». I also code and love cooking—that much that I published my first cookbook in 2023. It’s my secret second career.

When I’m not cooking I rely on my recipe for great User Experiences: User-centric concepts designed excellently and told with captivating stories. By following these three principles I aim to achieve what we all want: products that are fun, usable, precise and easy to use.
